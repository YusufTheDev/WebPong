WebPong

Web Pong Game Instructions

Objective

Reach a score of 15 to win the game.

How to Play

  1. Start the Game: Click on the "Enter Queue" button to search for an opponent to go against.
  2. Controls:
    • Use the W key to move the paddle up.
    • Use the S key to move the paddle down.
  3. Gameplay: The ball will automatically serve from the center. Use your paddle to hit the ball towards your opponent. Score a point when your opponent fails to return the ball.
  4. Paddle Controls: To aim the angle of the ball, hit the ball towards the top of the paddle to aim upwards and at the bottom of the paddle to aim downwards.
  5. Winning the Game: The first player to reach 15 points wins the game. The game will automatically end and display the winner.

Multiplayer

Share the website link with your friend. Your friend can queue up on their browser to vs you.

Additional Tips

Make sure to keep moving as if you remain stationary for over 30 seconds, you will be disconnected and as such forfeit the match.

Keep your paddle moving to anticipate the ball's direction. Aim your shots to make it difficult for your opponent to return.

Enjoy the game and may the best player win!
